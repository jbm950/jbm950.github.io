>> x component: $Z_{x} = Zcos(\theta)$  
>> y component: $Z_{y} = Zsin(\theta)$

> __Magnitude and Angle from Components__

>> Magnitude: $|Z| = \sqrt{Z_{x}^2 + Z_{y}^{2}}$  
>> Angle: $\theta = tan^{-1}(\frac{Z_{x}}{Z_{y}})$
