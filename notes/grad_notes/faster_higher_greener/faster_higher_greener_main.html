Optimal Control

  • Often posed in the general Bolza form
    • Minimize the objective functional by determining the following
      • The state of the system (x(t))
      • The control for the system (u(t))
      • The initial time ()
      • The terminal time ()
    • Objective functional subject to:
      • Dynamic constraint
      • Path constraint
      • Boundary condition
  • Bolza problem leads to first-order calculus of variations conditions
